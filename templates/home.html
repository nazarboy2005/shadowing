Shadowing is a proven technique for improving language skills, particularly pronunciation and fluency. According to a study published in the Language Learning Journal, students who practiced shadowing showed significant improvements in their speaking abilities compared to traditional methods.

Shadowing: Who benefits and how? Uncovering a booming EFL teaching technique for listening comprehension. Language Teaching Research, 20(1), 35-52.)
